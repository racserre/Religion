My Favorite Titles of Christ

Great High Priest

I loved learning about this title because although it seems like Christ should be untouchable and the most pure since He is the Great High Priest, He descended to the lowest depths imaginable to offer salvation from our sins. Although priests needed to be clean and pure, Christ took upon Himself all of our sins, becoming the most unclean priest. He suffered for all of us so that we all can enter the Holy of Holies. He is also personal. When He visited the Nephites, He was touched intimately and personally for hours even though He was the Great High Priest.

Jehovah

Seeing Christ as Yahweh, His “presentness”, has made me become even more away of His presence. He is always with us even when we feel like He isn't. He is exactly where He needs to be and I have seen that in my life. During the bad days, there are always blessings and we can see the hand of God in our lives.

Advocate

Christ speaks on behalf of us and because of Him, we can return to live with God again. I loved how we discussed the Intercessory Prayer and how Christ prayed on behalf of us. He proved how He was worthy to be our advocate. Christ is the perfect example and shows us how we should pray for others as well.

Redeemer

A redeemer or goel was understood to be the kinsman responsible to redeem the lost family member. I loved learning about this role of Christ and how He really is our loving brother that is always looking out for us. He redeems us from sin and brings us back when we lose our way.
