My Testimony

I know Jesus Christ is the Savior of the world and is my personal Savior. I know that He sacrificed His life for us and that He lives. I will be forever grateful for what He did for me so that I can be clean of my sins and return to live with my Heavenly Father again. Throughout this class, I have learned that Christ really is the King of Kings yet He is so personal to everyone and goes down to their level. He cares for every single person and will find the "lost sheep" even if it is just a single sheep out of 99. Although I will never be perfect like Him, He gives me the possibility and the hope to be. He is the perfect example that I will always try to follow since the path He made is for our benefit and so we can be happy in this life and in the life to come.
